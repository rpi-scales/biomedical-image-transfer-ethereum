Certificate Registry

Welcome to the "Certificate Registry" DApp. This distributed app runs on the Ethereum blockchain network and holds a registry of Certificates as Solidity smart contract.
  • The registry keeps images of Certificates along with their publish date.
  • Contract owner can submit new Certificates to be stored on the blockchain.
  • Users can view the existence of certain Certificates in the registry.

Submit a Certificate

Contract owners can register (upload) new Certificates to the "Certificate Registry" smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain decentralized network.


View Certificates

Blockchain users can view Certificates in the registry on the Ethereum network.
